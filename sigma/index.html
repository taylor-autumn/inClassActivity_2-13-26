9 Text Protoypes: Type A - "GLITCH"

This is one of the three words I chose out the 9 iteration assignment for part 1 of Assignment 2: Iterative Text. I was brainstorming words that I thought would be good canvas's for interaction, as well as browsing fun fonts to go along with them, when I came across this glitch font and got the idea to make the text centered around the idea of a computer glitch. For each of these iterations, I thought of ways to make the word seem like an authentic computer glitch, and for ways to have their movement represent this glitch.
